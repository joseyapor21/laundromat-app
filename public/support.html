Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
Contact the store administrator to reset your password. They can update it through the Admin panel.
How do I enable/disable push notifications?
Go to the Profile tab in the app and toggle the "Push Notifications" switch.
How do I connect a Bluetooth printer?
Go to Admin > Printers tab, scan for available devices, and tap to connect to your printer.
I'm having trouble logging in
Make sure you're using the correct email and password. If you've forgotten your password, contact your administrator for a reset.
How do I report a bug?
Please email us at jose.gyapor@gmail.com with a description of the issue and any screenshots if possible.
